Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use this website:

  2. Email Newsletter: If you subscribe to our weekly email newsletter, we collect your email address to send you the latest updates and blog posts. You may opt-out at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in the newsletter email.

  5. Third-Party Services: We do not share your personal information with third-party services unless required by law or for essential functionality (e.g., email newsletter management services).
  6. Data Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no data transmission or storage system is 100% secure.
  7. Changes to This Policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
